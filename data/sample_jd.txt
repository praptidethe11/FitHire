Senior Machine Learning Engineer

- Product company experience strongly preferred (not services/consulting)

Nice to have:

- MLOps tooling: MLflow, Weights and Biases, Kubeflow
- Background in recommendation systems or search ranking

Notice period: Prefer 30 days or less. Hybrid work from Bangalore.
